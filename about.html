About

This webpage provides essential contact information for various entities related to terminal operations. It serves as a comprehensive directory, encompassing contact numbers for terminal operations staff, internal numbers, airline numbers, resorts, resort representatives, guesthouses in Male, Hulhumale, and various islands.

Furthermore, this page also offers the layout and corresponding counter numbers for the resorts situated in the TRC (Tourist Representative Counter Area). By providing this information, the webpage aims to facilitate efficient communication and coordination within the terminal operations environment, ensuring smooth interactions and assistance for visitors, guests, and staff members alike.
